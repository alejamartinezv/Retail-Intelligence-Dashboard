# 🚀 Retail Intelligence & District Management Dashboard

> Retail leaders need visibility into sales performance, inventory availability and execution quality to identify growth opportunities and improve field productivity.

This dashboard provides an integrated view of key operational KPIs, helping leaders make faster and smarter business decisions.

---

## 📋 Project Overview
This project bridges the gap between complex operational data and executive decision-making. In the retail industry, success depends on the perfect harmony between sales performance, inventory availability, and flawless store execution.

This dashboard provides a 360° view of the operation, allowing leaders to identify growth opportunities and operational bottlenecks in real-time.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology

*   **Data Source:** Multi-sheet raw operational data reflecting real-world retail complexity.
*   **ETL Process:** Full **Power Query** workflow for data cleaning, normalization, and transformation.
*   **Modeling:** Implementation of **Star Schemas** and advanced **DAX** measures for time-intelligence and productivity KPIs.
*   **UX/UI Design:** High-contrast **Dark Mode** interface designed for executive focus and reduced cognitive load.

## 📖 Glossary of Metrics
<details>
  <summary>Click to expand technical definitions</summary>

*   **Sell-Out:** Total sales made to the end consumer at the store level.
*   **YoY (Year over Year):** Performance comparison against the same period in the previous year.
*   **Fill Rate:** Supply chain efficiency; the percentage of ordered items that actually reached the shelf.
*   **DOH (Days On Hand):** Inventory turnover metric indicating how many days current stock will last.
*   **Planogram Compliance:** Measure of how accurately products are displayed according to the brand's visual strategy.
</details>
